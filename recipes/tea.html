Tea

Description

Tea is one of the most loved beverage in India and even abroad. Whether it's morning or an evening snack-time, a cup of hot masala tea is a must to freshen up and energize the body. Just pair it with hot samosas or pakode and you're in for a delicious snack meal.

Ingredients

  • Cloves
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnamon
  • Water
  • Tea leaves
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Roast the spices like cloves, cinnamon, cardamom and star anise together in equal amounts in a pan.
  2. Grind the roasted ingredients together to get a powder.
  3. In a wok full of boiling water, put the ground mixture along with the other remaining ingredients.
  4. Stir and bring it to simmer.
  5. Serve hot along with some tea-time munchies.
